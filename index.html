Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need travel insurance?

We always recommend it for peace of mind. Travel insurance protects you against unexpected events like trip cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost luggage.

Can you help with group bookings?

Yes, from families to corporate trips. We specialize in group travel and can arrange special rates and customized itineraries for groups of all sizes.

Do you charge planning fees?

Most services are complimentary. We earn our commission from our travel partners, so our planning services come at no additional cost to you.
