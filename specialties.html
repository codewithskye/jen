Ethical Travel Tips

How you can make a positive impact while traveling

Support Local Businesses

Choose locally-owned restaurants, shops, and tour operators. Your spending directly benefits the community and helps preserve local culture and traditions.

Conserve Resources

Be mindful of water and energy consumption. Reuse towels, take shorter showers, and turn off lights and air conditioning when leaving your room.

Reduce Waste

Bring reusable water bottles, bags, and utensils. Avoid single-use plastics and dispose of waste properly, following local recycling guidelines.

Respect Local Culture

Learn about local customs, dress codes, and etiquette. Show respect for religious sites, traditions, and local ways of life.

Photograph Responsibly

Always ask permission before photographing people, especially in traditional or religious settings. Respect privacy and cultural sensitivities.

Choose Eco-Friendly Options

Select accommodations and activities with environmental certifications. Participate in conservation activities and support wildlife protection efforts.
