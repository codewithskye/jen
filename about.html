Our Journey

A timeline of milestones and achievements

2014 - The Beginning

Jen started her journey in the travel industry with a passion for helping others explore the world. Beginning as a travel consultant, she quickly developed a reputation for exceptional service and attention to detail.

2017 - Ethical Travel Certification

Recognizing the importance of responsible tourism, Jen became a certified ethical travel agent, specializing in sustainable and community-based travel experiences.

2019 - Epic Adventures by Jen

Launched Epic Adventures by Jen as an independent travel agency, focusing on personalized service and ethical travel practices. Quickly gained a loyal client base through word-of-mouth referrals.

2021 - Digital Expansion

Adapted to the changing travel landscape by expanding digital services, offering virtual consultations and enhanced online booking experiences while maintaining the personal touch.

2024 - Continued Growth

Today, Epic Adventures by Jen continues to grow, serving hundreds of satisfied travelers while maintaining our commitment to ethical tourism and exceptional service.
