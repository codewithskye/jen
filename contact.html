Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How much does it cost to use your services?

Our planning services are completely free! We earn our commission from our travel partners, so there's no cost to you for our expertise and personalized service. You'll often save money through our exclusive deals and group rates.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For the best selection and pricing, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for domestic trips and 6-12 months for international travel. However, we can also help with last-minute bookings and often find great deals!

Do you handle travel insurance?

Yes! We strongly recommend travel insurance and can help you choose the right coverage for your trip. We work with reputable insurance providers to ensure you're protected against unexpected events.

What if I need to change or cancel my trip?

We're here to help with any changes or cancellations. Policies vary by supplier, but we'll work with you to minimize fees and find the best solution. This is another reason why travel insurance is so valuable!

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! Many of our travel packages offer flexible payment options. You can often secure your booking with a deposit and pay the balance closer to your travel date. We'll explain all payment options during the planning process.

What makes you different from booking online myself?

We provide personalized service, insider knowledge, exclusive deals, and 24/7 support. If something goes wrong during your trip, you have a dedicated advocate working to resolve issues quickly. Plus, our expertise often saves you time and money!
